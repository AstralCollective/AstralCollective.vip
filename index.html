FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept demos from unsigned artists? >

Yes! We are always looking for new talent. Please submit your demo through a link to your portfolio or tracks. We listen to everything, but due to volume, we only respond to artists we select to move forward with.

What support do you offer your artists? >

We provide a full-spectrum of support including marketing, mentorship, licensing opportunities, and brand partnerships to help you focus on your craft and build a sustainable career.

What is Astral Collective? >

Astral Collective is a music discovery and artist development agency dedicated to finding unique and untapped musical talent and connecting them with impactful opportunities, including placements with brands and studios.

Do you handle music licensing? >

Absolutely. We manage synchronization and master use licensing for our artists' catalogs, connecting their tracks to film, television, games, and advertising projects worldwide. This is a key part of our service.

How do brand partnerships work? >

We actively seek out authentic brand alignment opportunities that match your artistic vision and values. We handle the entire process—from pitching and negotiation to campaign execution and reporting.

Do I maintain ownership of my masters? >

Maintaining artist creative control is key. While deal structures vary, we prioritize our artists retaining the maximum amount of ownership of their masters and publishing possible.
